My Story

I am a Carnegie Mellon University & Qatar Foundation computer science student who wanted to have a super simple way to find about movies in Qatar, even when I am offline. So, I integrated the whole movie showtimes & details, booking (for some cinemas), and navigating/getting an Uber ride experience in one app with less than 5 clicks from opening the app to being in the cinema!
